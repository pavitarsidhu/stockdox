About StockDox

StockDox provides viewing access to all the important filings that pertain to Canadian public companies. We have filings for companies that are listed on the TSX (Toronto Stock Exchange) and the TSXV (TSX-Venture Exchange).

Goal

StockDox aims to simplify the hassle behind trying to gather the various relevant documents that you may need for any respective Canadian company. Utlimately, this will allow you to focus on more core tasks and save time. This will be useful for both retail and institutional investors alike.
